Introducing Doppl Aware™

The Industry Standard For Asset Tracking and Level Detection.

Length: 8.50cm            Height: 4.50cm

Connectivity & Tracking

Doppl Aware™ is compatible with LTE-M1 or NB-IOT networks for global cellular connectivity. Using up to 6 different satellite networks, Doppl Aware™ manages the location of your assets with unprecedented reliability and +/- 1 meter accuracy.

Level Detection

Doppl Aware™ uses a multi-point infrared laser to generate a 3D surface of inside the container. Our software algorithms accounts will automatically determine container size and compute an average fill-level with under 5mm accuracy.

Temperature Detection

Doppl Aware™ reports temperatures with 0.01°C precision. When there are unusual temperature changes, receive instant notifications with the Doppl Dashboard™.

Rugged Design

Doppl Aware’s™ technology is protected by a highly engineered casing that offers uncompromising durability in the most extreme environments. Vibration, impact, water, temperature, and chemical resistance are at the core of the case’s design.

The Doppl Difference

Laser-Focus Accuracy

Eliminate abnormal level readings in metal tanks - a common issue with ultrasonic sensors. Suitable to detect clear liquids (i.e. water and refined fuels).

Waterproof (IP69K Rated)

Doppl Aware's™ IP69K rated casing is completely dust resistant and protected against close-range high pressure, high temperature spray downs.

Suitable for -50ºC

Designed for Canadian winters, the Doppl Aware™ suitable operating temperature is between -40ºC and 60ºC.

10+ Year Battery Life

Doppl Aware™ will last over 10 years with one reading every hour. The sensor lifespan may differ based on reading frequency and customer requirements.

Spark-Proof Design

Doppl Aware™ is spark and explosion proof, an essential for any hazardous and dangerous goods application. Ask us about our industry-specific certifications.

10 Year Warranty

In the unlikely event a Doppl sensor becomes damaged or unusable within 10 years, we will send a replacement completely free of charge.
